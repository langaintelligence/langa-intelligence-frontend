Privacy, clearly explained.

Langa is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 ("POPIA"). We collect only the information necessary to provide the Langa platform. We do not sell your personal information, share it for advertising purposes, or use your documents to train public AI models. By using Langa, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

Langa is a document intelligence and storage platform operated by Modise Jasina Projects (Pty) Ltd, a registered South African company.

For the purposes of POPIA, Modise Jasina Projects (Pty) Ltd is the Responsible Party responsible for the processing of personal information through the platform.

Contact Email: hello@langai.co.za
Operating Entity: Modise Jasina Projects (Pty) Ltd, South Africa

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information in order to provide and secure the platform:

We do not store biometric information, government-issued identity numbers, or payment card information found within uploaded documents. Any such personal information detected in document text is automatically removed before AI processing — your personal details never leave your secure account. Payment processing is handled securely by third-party payment providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for purposes related to operating the platform, including:

  • Providing document analysis and summaries
  • Displaying reminders and important dates
  • Managing authentication and account access
  • Sending transactional emails
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • Enforcing plan usage limits
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving platform reliability and performance

We do not sell your information, share your information for advertising, or use your documents for marketing purposes.

4. AI Processing and Document Analysis

Langa uses trusted third-party AI providers, including Anthropic Claude, to process uploaded documents and generate summaries, explanations, reminders, and document insights.

When you upload a document:

  • personal information detected in your document is automatically removed before any AI processing takes place
  • document text is then securely processed through third-party AI infrastructure with personal details removed
  • analysis is performed over encrypted connections
  • your uploaded files remain stored securely and are associated only with your private account
🔒 Your personal details are removed before AI processing

Personal information detected in uploaded documents — including SA ID numbers, phone numbers, email addresses, bank account numbers, physical addresses, and passport numbers — is automatically removed and replaced with neutral placeholders before document text is sent to our AI provider. Your original document stored on Langa is never modified. This means your personal details never leave your secure account.

ℹ️ AI data handling — what you should know

According to Anthropic's API policies at the time of writing, customer data submitted through the API is not used to train public AI models. Users are encouraged to review Anthropic's latest policies at anthropic.com/policies for updated information.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

Langa uses trusted third-party providers to operate parts of the platform, including cloud infrastructure providers, authentication services, payment processors, email delivery services, and AI processing providers.

These providers may process limited information strictly for the purpose of delivering platform functionality and services on our behalf.

We do not authorize third parties to sell or use your information for unrelated marketing purposes.
